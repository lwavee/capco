Deep Dive: Why Your Home Policy Isn't Enough for Your Boat

The Risk of "Incidental" Coverage

Many homeowners believe their boat is covered while it's in their driveway. While your home policy might offer a small amount of protection for a tiny skiff, it rarely covers liability on the water, fuel spill cleanup, or high-speed collision damage. A dedicated Marine Insurance policy is built for the specific risks of the aquatic environment.

Understanding Fuel Spill Liability

In California, if your boat sinks or leaks fuel, you are legally responsible for the cleanup costs, which can easily reach $50,000+. Most standard policies exclude this entirely. Our watercraft policies include dedicated fuel spill and wreck removal limits as standard features.

Discounts for Safe Boaters

Maintaining a clean driving record on land and completing a NASBLA-approved boater safety course can significantly reduce your marine premiums. We help you find every available credit to keep your fun on the water affordable.

Watercraft FAQs

Does it cover my trailer?

Yes. Physical damage to your trailer can be included in your policy, along with roadside assistance for trailer-related breakdowns.

Are personal watercraft (PWC) covered?

Yes. We have specialized programs for Jet Skis and WaveRunners, focusing on high-limit liability for these high-performance machines.

Does it cover towing?

Yes. Our premium policies include on-water towing to the nearest repair facility if you experience a mechanical failure.
