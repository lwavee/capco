Deep Dive: Motorcycle Insurance vs. Auto "Add-Ons"

The Hazard of Generic Coverage

Many big-name insurance companies treat motorcycles as a "line item" on an auto policy. This often leads to massive gaps in coverage for things like safety gear (helmets/leathers) or specialized towing that won't damage your bike's forks or frame. A dedicated Motorcycle Insurance policy ensures these bike-specific needs are met from day one.

Understanding Agreed Value for Customs

If you've spent $10,000 on custom chrome and performance parts, a standard policy will only pay you the "book value" of the base bike. We offer Agreed Value policies where we document your upgrades upfront, ensuring you get every penny back in the event of a total loss.

California Riding Laws & Liability

California has specific laws regarding lane splitting and helmet requirements. Our agents stay updated on these regulations to ensure your liability limits are high enough to protect you in one of the most litigious states in the country.

Motorcycle FAQs

Does it cover my helmet?

Yes. Our specialized policies include up to $3,000 in safety apparel coverage, which includes helmets, jackets, and boots.

What if I only ride in the summer?

We offer "Layup" policies where coverage is reduced during off-months to save you money, while still protecting against theft and fire.

Can I insure my custom paint?

Absolutely. We can add a specialized endorsement for custom paint and chrome work to match your actual investment.
