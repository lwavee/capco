Deep Dive: Understanding Your Home Policy

Replacement Cost vs. Actual Cash Value

In California, construction costs can fluctuate wildly. We always recommend Replacement Cost coverage for both your dwelling and your personal property. This ensures you can actually rebuild your home and replace your belongings at today's prices, rather than receiving a depreciated payout that leaves you short.

The Role of Liability Protection

Liability insurance is one of the most overlooked parts of a homeowners policy. It protects you if someone is injured on your property (like a slip-and-fall) or if you're sued for damage you accidentally cause to others. We recommend at least $300,000 in liability, often paired with an Umbrella Policy for maximum protection.

Home Owners FAQs

Is flood insurance included?

No. Standard home policies exclude flood damage. We can provide a separate Flood Insurance quote through the NFIP or private carriers.

What is "Scheduled Personal Property"?

It's extra coverage for high-value items like engagement rings or fine art that exceed the standard limits of your policy.

How can I lower my premium?

Upgrading your roof, installing a smart security system, and bundling with your auto insurance are great ways to save.
