Frequently Asked Questions

What types of commercial insurance do I need for my small business?

Most small businesses in California require at least General Liability and Workers Compensation. Depending on your industry, you might also need Professional Liability (E&O) or a Business Owners Policy (BOP).

How can I lower my insurance premiums?

Bundling your auto and home insurance, maintaining a good claims history, and increasing your deductibles are all effective ways to reduce your annual premiums.
