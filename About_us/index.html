About Capital & Co
Insurance Services

Capital & Co Insurance Services has been offering specialized commercial insurance solutions in Oregon, California, and Washington for over 15 years. We focus on delivering affordable insurance packages tailored for contractors, small businesses, and professional services.

With licensed agents committed to your success, we operate across all major lines of coverage — including Workers' Compensation, General Liability, Commercial Auto, Professional Liability, and Contractor Bonds. At Capital & Co Insurance Services, we combine industry knowledge with trusted relationships and customer-first service.
